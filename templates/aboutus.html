This System Provide Markov Chain Analysis of Rainfall.

The yield of crops particularly under rainfed condition depends on the rainfall pattern. A simple criteria related to sequential phenomena like dry and wet spells could be used for analyzing rainfall data to obtain specific information needed for crop planning and for carrying out agricultural operations, Markov chain probability model has been recognized as a suitable model to explain the long term frequency behaviour of wet or dry spells. Several authors have demonstrated its practical utility in agricultural planning for both long and short term periods. This model enables to determine the probability of occurrence of dry and wet spells during a particular week.
